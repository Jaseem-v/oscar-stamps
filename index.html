FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order a seal or stamp? #1

To order a seal or stamp, simply visit our website, choose your desired product, customize it with your details, and place your order. You can also contact us directly for personalized assistance.

What types of seals and stamps do you offer? #2

We offer a wide range of seals and stamps including self-inking stamps, pre-inked stamps, rubber stamps, embossing seals, and electronic seals. Our products cater to various needs such as notary, corporate, and personal use.

How long does it take to receive my order? #3

Our standard processing time is 3-5 business days. However, expedited services are available for urgent requests. Delivery times may vary based on your location.

Do you offer bulk discounts? #6

Yes, we offer discounts on bulk orders. Please contact our sales team for more information and to get a quote based on your specific needs and order quantity.
